B is a base.
D is a decimal.

$$
D=d_{n} \times B^n + d_{n-1} \times B^{n-1} + \dots + d_{1} \times B^{1} + d_{0} \times B^0
$$

For example:
Convert the binary $100$ to decimal:
$$
D = 1 \times 2^{3-1} + 0 \times 2^{2-1} + 0 \times 2^{1-1} 
$$
