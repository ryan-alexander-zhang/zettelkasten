## Get the CA Chain

```shell
openssl s_client -connect ${HOST}:443 -servername ${HOST} -showcerts </dev/null 2>/dev/null \
  | awk '/BEGIN CERTIFICATE/{i++} {print > ("cert-" i ".pem")}'
```
