Open Source Intelligence (OSINT) enables professionals to gather and analyze information from public sources, including websites, social media platforms (such as Twitter), official records, and news outlets.

What are the 5 steps of OSINT?
* Planning
* Data collection
* Data processing
* Data analysis
* Reporting

Here's an [OSINT Framework](https://osintframework.com/).
